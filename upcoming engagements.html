Upcoming Engagements

Sunday, June 22 at 7:00pm

The Golden Age of Broadway. Ms. Adams joins a star-studded cast of Chicago area singers and the Elgin Symphony orchestra for a night an evening of showtunes at the beautiful Ravina Festival grounds. This marks Ms. Adams' debut at the Ravinia festival. Bring your picnic and your family out for an evening of classics under the stars. This will be one of the highlights of the summer!

Saturday, July 4 at 8:00pm

Heros of America. Ms. Adams joins the Milwaukee Symphony Orchestra and Chorus for an evening of music and fireworks. Held on the Lakefront at Milwaukee's beautiful Lake Park.

Saturday, September 12-Sunday, September 20

Ms. Adams will return to Louisville and reprise her role as the soceress in Orpheo ed Euridice with Bourbon Baroque. This fully staged production will mark new Artistic Director and principal violinist Alice Culin-Ellison's first full season with the ensemble.

Friday, December 12

Rockefeller Chapel Messiah is a Chicago Holiday tradition for many. Ms. Adams will again perform the alto solos under the baton of Maestro James Kalambach. Last year the Chicago Tribune praised Ms. Adams for her, "steller coloratura and breathtaking musicianship." This performance is not to be missed.
