Praised for her "remarkably clear singing and perfect phrasing" (Stage and Cinema) and her "Bell like purity" (Chicago Tribune), Lindsey Adams has established herself as one of the foremost musical interpretors of the day. She is in demand as a soloist throughout the country, as well as maintaining a busy performance calendar in the Chicago area, where she makes her home.

Also an accomplished teacher and music director. Ms. Adams is currently the director of music at Mary Seat of Wisdom Parish in Park Ridge, IL. Here she maintains an active vocal and piano studio with students of all ages, and directs 5 diverse ensembles of musicians.

Ms. Adams has self-published many of her original harmonizations, hymn arrangements, and original compositions written for the ensembles at Mary Seat of Wisdom. She has extensive experience directing and orchestrating both staged musical productions, classical oratorio, and childrens' theater pieces.
